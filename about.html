About Tori

The calm, thorough touch behind Northwest Wax.

Waxing since 2012 Esthetics school, 2011 Northwest Wax since 2018 Brazilian specialist

Northwest Wax is owned and operated by Tori Gledhill. Tori was born and raised in Phoenix, AZ but always dreamed of moving to the Pacific Northwest. She has lived in Oregon since 2015. She enjoys reading, being out in nature and hanging out with her fiancé and 2 kitties, Tiny and Boo!

Tori has been waxing professionally since 2012 and has performed thousands of services during that time. She completed Esthetics School in 2011 in Arizona. After working for a waxing chain for many years, she finally started Northwest Wax in 2018 and went off on her own. She offers full body waxing in Portland/Beaverton and specializes in brazilian wax services. Her gentle touch, calm demeanor and thoroughness will keep you coming back month after month.
